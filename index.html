Who we are

Sensory Wonderland, the first state-of-art kid’s sensory gym in the mid-south region, is specially designed for children with autism, sensory processing disorders, ADHD/ADD or other developmental disabilities.  It is a family friendly facility providing families with a secure, safe, therapeutic and fun environment for children with special needs to improve physical strength, sensory processing, communication, social skills, and self-help skills.  Our facility features climbing walls, swings, a slide, ball pits, crash pads, zip lines, sand box/sand tray and more to help children receive sensory input and gain self-awareness and self-confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high quality therapy services to children with special needs to meet their comprehensive needs while providing a safe, therapeutic, fun environment where children can learn through experience and exploration. In addition, our mission also includes supporting the family system by offering ancillary services (psychoeducational groups and coaching) to siblings and caregivers.
